Herbal compounds that provide anti-inflammatory effects and inhibit androgen receptors.

Criteria for Target Application Classification

The "Target Application" column classifies patents based on their primary intended use and regulatory pathway, as described in the filings:

  • Cosmetic: For products intended for aesthetic purposes to manage unwanted hair, typically for over-the-counter consumer use.
  • Pharma/Cosmetic: For "cosmeceuticals" that bridge cosmetics and pharmaceuticals, addressing both aesthetic concerns and medical conditions like hirsutism.
  • Pharma: For distinctly medical applications aimed at treating a diagnosed dermatological disease where hair growth is a primary symptom. These are likely prescription-only treatments.
  • Pharma/Professional: A specialized category for pharmaceutical-grade treatments that require administration by a healthcare professional in a clinical setting (e.g., antibody-drug conjugates).
