Key Patents in Hair Growth Retardants

An analysis of unique patent families and their core inventions over the years.

Priority Year: 2025

Chemical & Natural

FAN: 114639304

Priority Date: 2025-04-25

Core Invention: Combines Eflornitine Hydrochloride with herbal phytochemicals like Curcumin, EGCG, and Apigenin. Synergistically inhibits 5a-reductase and ODC enzymes for stronger, more permanent hair reduction effects.

Key Active Ingredients:

Eflornitine Hydrochloride

Inhibits the Ornithine Decarboxylase (ODC) enzyme, slowing cell division in follicles.

Curcumin & Apigenin

Herbal compounds that provide anti-inflammatory effects and inhibit androgen receptors.

Priority Year: 2024

Chemical

FAN: 114087267

Priority Date: 2024-12-31

Core Invention: A novel depilatory compound created by conjugating Interferon IFNγ with an antibody that targets hair follicle stem cells or hair bulbs. This targeted delivery breaks the follicle's immune tolerance, leading to immune-mediated hair removal.

Key Active Ingredient:

Interferon IFNγ-Antibody Conjugate

Targets hair follicles and induces an immune response to achieve temporary or permanent depilation.

View Database Data
Natural

FAN: 110942797

Priority Date: 2024-05-16

Core Invention: A skin cosmetic composition featuring marquain, a compound found in extracts like Sophora Flavescens (Kujin). It inhibits the testosterone 5α-reductase enzyme, effectively suppressing the hormonal trigger for hair growth.

Key Active Ingredient:

Marquain

Inhibits the testosterone 5α-reductase enzyme to reduce hair growth promotion.

View Database Data
Natural

FAN: 109860381

Priority Date: 2024-05-09

Core Invention: An herbal depilatory gel peel-off mask formulated with papain and bromelain as key ingredients. These proteolytic enzymes break down hair proteins and weaken the follicle for gentle, effective hair removal.

Key Active Ingredients:

Papain

A proteolytic enzyme from papaya that disintegrates the hair cuticle.

Bromelain

An enzyme from pineapple that acts as a natural exfoliant and helps soften hair.

Natural

FAN: 110949151

Priority Date: 2024-01-24

Core Invention: Composition with Eurya Japonica extract. Inhibits dermal papilla cells. Reduces hair growth factors like ALP. Effectively retards hair growth for cosmetic or pharmaceutical use.

Key Active Ingredient:

Eurya Japonica Extract

Inhibits dermal papilla cell development, thereby stopping the hair growth cycle.

FAN: 111582895

Priority Date: 2024-01-24

Key Active Ingredient:
